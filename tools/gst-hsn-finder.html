🔎 How to read a code

HSN codes classify goods (e.g. 0406 = cheese & paneer). SAC codes classify services and always begin with 99 (e.g. 9963 = hotel accommodation).

📋 How many digits to quote

Turnover up to ₹5 cr: 4-digit HSN on B2B invoices. Above ₹5 cr: 6-digit HSN on all invoices. SAC must be shown for all taxable services.

➗ CGST + SGST split

For supply within a state, GST splits equally into CGST + SGST. For inter-state supply, the full rate is charged as IGST.
